FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about Canonix DEX

What is Canonix?

Canonix is a next-generation decentralized exchange (DEX) built on the Paxi Network. It allows users to swap PRC20 tokens instantly, provide liquidity, and analyze markets using AI-powered tools.

Is Canonix non-custodial?

Yes. Canonix never takes control of your funds. You trade directly from your own wallet (Keplr, PaxiHub, or our secure Internal Wallet). Your private keys or mnemonics remain encrypted on your device.

What are the trading fees?

Standard swap fees on Canonix are highly competitive. Most of the fee goes to liquidity providers, with a small portion dedicated to protocol maintenance and development.

How does the AI Analysis work?

Our AI module uses Google's Gemini Pro to interpret real-time market data, including price trends, volume spikes, and RSI levels, providing you with technical summaries and sentiment analysis.

What is the "Internal Wallet"?

The Internal Wallet is a browser-based wallet that doesn't require extensions. It uses industry-standard AES-GCM encryption to store your mnemonic locally. It's protected by a 6-digit PIN you create.

Where can I see my transaction history?

You can view your recent transactions directly within the Trade terminal under the "History" tab, or by looking up your address on Winscan Explorer.
